Jennifer Nicolay

I am a Frankfurt-based artist, composer, educator, and digital developer working across composition, live electronics, performance, participatory artistic formats, and digital mediation. Her practice connects sound, code, pedagogy, curatorial thinking, and collaborative creation.

Composition Performance Pedagogy Curatorial Practice Digital Development

Profile

Her artistic work moves between contemporary music, interdisciplinary performance, electronic sound, and hybrid formats that combine acoustic instruments, digital tools, spatial thinking, and conceptual design. A particular focus lies in linking artistic experimentation with social relevance and educational practice.

Current Focus

Current focal points include contemporary music performance, live electronics, machine learning for artistic practice, collaborative concert dramaturgy, and music-and-electronics projects with children and young people. She develops artistic and pedagogical formats in which sound, movement, objects, code, and participation become equally important compositional elements.

Artistic Practice

Her work includes compositions, live electronic settings, performance concepts, educational workshop formats, curatorial collaboration, and digital development. Across these fields, she is interested in liminality, resonance, transformation, memory, and the aesthetic possibilities of technological mediation.
